**Activity #2 Interrupts**

1. Identify your main network card (NIC)

2. Look in realtime the interrupts related to your NIC

3. What could be the result of excessive network traffic?

5. Start a simple UDP server (use netcat, or create one simple server in C, or other...)

6. Check that the server is properly listening using standard linux command

7. Find out what is the process ID (PID) of your server

8. Get ``strace`` running against the process in another window

9. Get real-time interrupts stats running in another window

10. Connect to your server using a well known linux tool, you can also try to stress it, but the effects may not be really visible.
